Project Stormwing

The Premier African-led AI Cybersecurity Initiative


In a world of plight and uncertainty, Africa needs to rise up with renewed guile and vigour against the nascent AI-aided threats of today's molting threat actors — with bile, sweat and blood. Inspired by Anthropic's Glasswing Initiative, Project Stormwing aims to fight fire with fire, rising up and staring down the rogue AI agents that have been proliferating on our networks.

Project Stormwing intends to create a safe cyberspace — for Africans, by Africans — by leveraging our own proprietary cybersecurity AI model to hunt down vulnerabilities in our infrastructure, the greater Pan-African environment and eventually encompassing the whole wide world. Moving forward with the project, the Project Stormwing team, harnessing the capabilities of our AI model, aims to exponentially increase the speed and efficiency at which vulnerabilities are detected, and patched.
